OUR CORE OBJECTIVES

i) Popularize all aspects of social enterprise for human advancement with thrust on holistic approach

ii) Work towards main-streaming backward communities with particular reference to tribal communities through innovative interventions

iii) Cater to the strategic needs of vulnerable sections of the society namely, children, women, old and physically challenged persons

iv) Provide an appropriate response to the fallen humanity through intense study of human life complexities,cultural biases and assumptions

v) Help the poor recovering their marred identity and vocation and helping the non-poor discovering their place in God’s redemptive plan – setting people free

vi) Present the Good News of the Kingdom of God as an agent of true social transformation and the creator of new identity
